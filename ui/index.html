Kheer

  • Time: 30 mins
  • Serves: 4 persons
Ingredients Quantity
slightly overcooked rice 1 cup
milk 2 cup
sugar 3 tablespoon
raisins 1 tablespoon
pistachios 1 tablespoon
saffron saffron
chopped almonds 1 tablespoon
powdered cardamom 1/8 tablespoon

Step 1: Put the raisins in a little bit of water to puff them up.

Step 2: Heat the pan over a medium gas flame or electric stove setting

Step 3: While the pan is heating up, grind the saffron and a pinch of sugar with a mortar and pestle. Set it to the side

Step 4: Add the cup of slightly overcooked rice and 2 cups of whole milk to your heated pan. Lightly stir this mixture.

Step 5: Add your mixture of saffron and sugar to the milk and rice in the pan.

Step 6: Add the sugar before the raisins and mixed nuts.

Step 7: Drain the raisins and add them to the mixture along with the 1 tablespoon chopped pistachios and 1 tablespoon chopped almonds.

Step 8: Remove the pan from the heat and add the 1/8 teaspoon powdered cardamom.

Besan Ladoo

  • Time: 16mins
  • Serves: 10 large ladoos
Ingredients Quantity
coarse besan 2 cup
ghee 1/2 to 2/3 cups
powdered sugar 1 cup
cardamom powder 1 tablespoon

Step 1: Roast the besan over medium-low heat

Step 2: Melt and add the ghee.

Step 3: Add cardamom and milk

Step 4: Let it cool in a mixing bowl

Step 5: Mix in powdered sugar

Step 6: Shape into balls.

Jalebi

  • Time: 50mins
  • Serves: 4 people
Ingredients Quantity
all-purpose flour 1 cup
gram 2 tablespoons
plain yogurt 3/4 cup
cardamom powder 1 tablespoon
baking soda 1/2 teaspoon
melted ghee 2 tablespoon
saffron 1/4 tsp

Step 1: Mix up the batter

Step 2: Adjust the thickness of the batter.

Step 3: Leave the batter to ferment.

Step 4: Make the batter

Step 5: Adjust the batter, if needed.

Step 6: Set the batter aside for 15 minutes..

Step 5: Boil the syrup.

Step 5: Test the consistency of the syrup.

Step 5: Remove the syrup from the heat.

Step 5: Heat the oil.

Step 5: Load the batter into a dispenser while the oil is heating.

Step 5: Pour some of the batter into the oil.

Step 5: Fry the jalebi until they are crisp and golden brown.

Step 5: Soak the jalebi in the syrup.

Step 5: Remove the jalebi from the syrup and serve.
